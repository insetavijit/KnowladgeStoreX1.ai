# 1.2.1.7 Global Styles & Presets

**Overview**
**Global Styles** are the "Design System" of a block theme. Defined in `theme.json`, they allow developers to set site-wide defaults for typography, colors, and layout. **Presets** are the specific options (color palettes, font sizes, gradients) available to users in the editor. By configuring these correctly, you ensure that even when users customize their site, they stay within the "guardrails" of your design language.
