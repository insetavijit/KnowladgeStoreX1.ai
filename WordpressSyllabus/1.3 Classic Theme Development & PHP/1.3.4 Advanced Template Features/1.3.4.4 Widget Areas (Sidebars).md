**Overview**  
Widget areas, also known as sidebars, are theme-defined regions that host modular blocks of content called widgets. By registering sidebars, themes provide site administrators with a drag-and-drop interface for managing layout components like navigation, social feeds, and custom content. Modern theme development requires understanding how to balance classic PHP-defined sidebars with the block-based widget editor, ensuring proper accessibility markers, and implementing efficient conditional logic to hide or show sidebars based on the page context.
