**Overview**  
Theme performance is the measurement of how quickly and efficiently a WordPress theme executes and renders for its users. In a world where milliseconds impact bounce rates and SEO rankings, performance optimization is a core skill for professional developers. This involves identifying bottlenecks in PHP execution, minimizing the number of database queries, and optimizing the critical rendering path. From efficient loop construction to smart asset management and proper use of the WordPress hook system, performance optimization ensures that a theme provides a smooth user experience even on low-powered devices or high-traffic servers.
