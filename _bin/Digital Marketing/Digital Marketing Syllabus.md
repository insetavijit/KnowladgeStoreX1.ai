### **Certification Priority Roadmap**

**Tier 1 (Essential - Get First):**

1. **Google Analytics 4 Certification** - Free, ~3-4 hours study, essential for most roles
2. **Google Ads Search Certification** - Free, required for PPC roles
3. **HubSpot Inbound Marketing** - Free, widely recognized, excellent fundamentals

**Tier 2 (Valuable - Get Within 3 Months):** 4. **Google Ads Display Certification** - Free, complements search 5. **HubSpot Content Marketing** - Free, valuable for content roles 6. **Meta Blueprint Certifications** - Free, important for social media roles

**Tier 3 (Specialized - Based on Career Path):** 7. **Google Shopping Ads** (if e-commerce focus) 8. **YouTube Advertising** (if video marketing focus) 9. **HubSpot Email Marketing** (if email focus) 10. **SEMrush/Ahrefs Academy** (if SEO focus)

**Timeline:** Complete Tier 1 before job applications; Tier 2 within first 3 months of study; Tier 3 based on specialization.
